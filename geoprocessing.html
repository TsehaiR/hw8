Impacted Watersheds

The ingestion exposure pathway indicated the area around a nuclear power plant that can expect to be affected by radiation particles in the event of a disaster at a nuclear plant. This disaster may potentially affect drinking water. This map shows suface water sheds within a 50 mile radius of a nuclear plant. From this map it can be determined that watersheds around central NC are greatly impacted, while watersheds in the mountains of North Carolina are largely unaffected.
